When budget items are manually added, edited, or deleted, the backend recalculates `project_budgets.totalEstimated`, but the older summary field `projects.budget` may not be updated.

Fix:
Whenever budget item create/update/delete recalculates `project_budgets.totalEstimated`, also update:

projects.budget = project_budgets.totalEstimated

Routes affected:

* POST /api/projects/:projectId/budget/items
* PATCH /api/projects/:projectId/budget/items/:itemId
* DELETE /api/projects/:projectId/budget/items/:itemId

* Keep project_budgets.totalEstimated as the source of truth for the Budget tab.
* Keep projects.budget synced for project summaries/cards/dashboards.
* Do not change totalActual.
* Do not add formulas.
