Goal:
Clean up Super Admin navigation, company/contractor sidebar ordering/default landing, and contractor dashboard report export scope.

* Remove the “Admin Tools” sidebar/nav item from Super Admin.
* Add a direct “Sandbox” nav item in the Super Admin sidebar/nav.
* Keep Role Definitions as its own existing nav item.
* Do not remove the Sandbox page/route itself.
* Do not build contractor-side sandbox in this phase.

Suggested label:
“Sandbox”

Done looks like:

* Super Admin sidebar has a direct Sandbox item.
* Admin Tools is gone.
* Role Definitions still exists separately.
* Existing Sandbox page still opens.

Issue:

Expected behavior:

* Move Admin Center near the bottom of the company/contractor sidebar list.
* Keep it accessible for Company Owner and Company Admin users.
* Do not remove Admin Center.
* Do not change permissions.

Done looks like:

4. Contractor Dashboard report/export scope

Issue:
The Contractor Dashboard export/report currently appears to export only projects where `project.contractorId === current user id`. That may be okay for a regular internal contractor/team member, but Company Owners and Company Admins likely expect company-wide project export.

Expected behavior:
Define export/report scope by role:

* Company Owner:

  * export all projects for their company

* Company Admin:

  * export all projects for their company

* Regular internal contractor/team member:

  * export projects assigned to them / allowed for their access, matching current behavior if that is the intended access model

* Client:

  * no internal contractor report access

* Subcontractor / Notary:

  * no internal company-wide report access unless a future assigned-scope workflow is built

* Platform/Super Admin:

  * keep existing admin behavior if already separate; do not broaden unless needed

Tasks:

* Inspect Contractor Dashboard export/report logic.
* Likely file:

  * `client/src/pages/contractor-dashboard.tsx`
  * backend routes/storage if the export is server-side
* Update the filter so Company Owner/Admin users export company-wide projects.
* Keep regular contractor/team member export scoped to their assigned/allowed projects.
* Preserve company scoping.
* Do not expose projects from other companies.

Also clarify naming if needed:
If the export is currently just a CSV list of projects, keep it simple. Do not build full reporting in this phase.

Done looks like:

* Company Owner/Admin export includes all company projects.
* Regular internal contractor export remains scoped appropriately.
* No cross-company project leakage.
* No new full reporting system is added.
