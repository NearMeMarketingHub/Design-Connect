Phase 11C permission correction: allow all internal company contractors to use Estimator/Quotes.

Context:
The Price Book is where company owners/company admins manage company pricing and imported budget sheet items.
The Estimator/Quote system is where internal company users use those items to create estimates/quotes.

Current issue:
Estimator and the Price Book picker may be limited to company_owner or isCompanyAdmin users only.

Desired model:

* All internal contractors/team members within the same company should be able to access and use the Estimator/Quote system.
* All internal contractors/team members within the same company should be able to read price book items inside the Estimator.
* Only company_owner and company admins should manage/edit/import Price Book items.

Do not change BuildVision branding.
Do not add Stripe work.
Do not add schema changes.
Do not add formulas.
Do not change estimate save payload.
Do not build new Price Book management UI.
Do not expand subcontractor/notary functionality.

1. Define Estimator/Quote access.

Allow these users to access /company/estimates and quote/estimator functionality:

* company_owner
* contractor users with companyId
* company admins with companyId

Block:

* clients
* subcontractors
* notaries
* users without companyId
* users from another company
* unauthenticated users

2. Allow read-only Price Book access for Estimator users.

The Estimator’s “From Price Book” picker should be available to internal company contractors/team members.

Update read-only price book GET routes if needed:

* GET /api/company/price-book/categories
* GET /api/company/price-book/items

Allowed for these GET routes:

* company_owner
* contractor users with companyId
* company admins with companyId

Blocked:

* clients
* subcontractors
* notaries
* users without companyId
* users from another company

These routes must continue filtering strictly by req.user.companyId.

3. Keep Price Book management restricted.

Do not expand write/manage access.

These should remain company_owner/company admin only:

* create price book categories
* edit price book categories
* delete price book categories
* create price book items
* edit price book items
* delete price book items
* import/bulk upload price book items
* any price book management settings

Regular contractors can use price book items in estimates, but cannot manage the company price book.

4. Update frontend Estimator picker permissions.

In client/src/pages/estimator.tsx:

* update canAccessPriceBook to allow internal company contractors/team members with companyId
* keep clients, subcontractors, notaries, and users without companyId blocked
